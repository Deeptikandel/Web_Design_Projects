Volunteering

Volunteers (dog walkers, etc.) are an important part of the Dogs Trust team.We have a wonderful team of full-time dedicated canine carers at our rescue Centre, but with so many dogs to look after an extra pair of hands is often much appreciated! If you're interested in giving us the very valuable gift of your time, please contact the rescue Centre. If you're too far from a Centre, there are also other ways to contact our rescue center. Please fill up the form to get your name nominated in a volunteer list. Your interest and support towards our oranization will be appriciated.
